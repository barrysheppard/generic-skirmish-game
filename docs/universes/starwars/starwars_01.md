# **Setting Information** {#setting-information}

## **Template for a normal Human** {#template-for-a-normal-human}

Below is the profile for a typical human. This will also apply for the majority of aliens.

| Name | M | WS | BS | Def | W | Cost |
| :---- | :---: | :---: | :---: | :---: | :---: | :---: |
| Average Human | 4 | 3 | 3 | 3 | 12 | 40 |


The Sprint ability is a \[double\] which adds 1” to the fighter’s movement for the rest of the turn. This trait gets removed if a fighter has any armour. Light armour will increase Def by 1, Heavy armour will increase Def by 2, unlike with other settings heavy armour is assumed to be powered so no \-1 Mv is required. More experienced fighters will have better Weapon Skill and Wounds.

The unarmed attack for standard human is below :

| Weapon | Rng | Att | Dmg | Traits |
| :---- | :---: | :---: | :---: | :---- |
| **Unarmed** | 0-1 | 2 | 1/3 |  |

So an unarmed human is doing 1.3 damage. This might be with a dagger, a rock, or a bit of wood. 

## **Template for typical Weapons** {#template-for-typical-weapons}

This setting is ranged heavy, so often fighters will be equipped with a blaster pistol or carbine.

| Weapon | Rng | Att | Dmg | Traits |
| :---- | :---: | :---: | :---: | :---- |
| **DH-17 Blaster Pistol** | 0-10 | 3 | 2/3 |  |
| **E-11 Blaster Rifle**  | 3-15 | 3 | 2/4 | Rapid Fire |

A blaster pistol then averages out to about 2.5 damage per attack and a blaster rifle is 3\. The Rapid Fire ability is a \[double\] that lets you increase the Attack dice for the turn by 1 which would bring the blaster rifle up to 4 average damage per attack. With that 15 inch range a fighter in the centre of the board will be able to shoot almost every part of the board. This will make a dense board and cover very important. Multiple fighters turning on a single exposed target will bring that target down pretty fast.

A pistol is likely to be shorter range, but will have a shorter minimum range and can be used in melee. This means a Rebel with a pistol will be at a disadvantage at the start compared to the Stormtrooper with blaster rifle, but once they close with that 3” minimum range the pistol will have an advantage.
