# Rebel Troopers

## **Rebel Sleeper Cell** {#rebel-sleeper-cell}

Rebel Sleeper Cells are covert resistance units embedded in Imperial territory. Often disguised as civilians or workers, they operate in secrecy until called to action. When activated, these fighters strike with ruthless precision.

| Fighter | Mv | Def | WS | W | Gear/Traits | Cr |
| :---- | :---: | :---: | :---: | :---: | :---- | :---: |
| **Rebel Operative** | 4 | 3 | 4 | 10 | Twin DH-17 Blaster Pistols, Cover Identity, Demolition Charge Pack, Sprint | 120 |
| **Rebel Operative** | 4 | 3 | 4 | 10 | A295 Sniper Rifle, Cover Identity, Demolition Charge Pack, Sprint | 120 |
| **Cell Leader** | 4 | 3 | 4 | 12 | Twin DH-17 Blaster Pistols, Cover Identity, Inspire Rebellion, Sprint, Coordinated Uprising | 130 |

Sleeper Cell Operatives are equipped with a pair of DH-17 Blaster Pistols. If the warband includes at least three operatives, one must be designated as the Cell Leader. If the warband includes four or more operatives, one may replace their pistols with a Sniper Rifle.
