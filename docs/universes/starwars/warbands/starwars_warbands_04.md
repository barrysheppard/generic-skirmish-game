# Imperial Heroes


## **Iden Versio** {#iden-versio}

Iden Versio is an elite Imperial commander and the leader of Inferno Squad, the Empire’s most feared special forces unit. Trained from a young age in combat, espionage, and tactics, she exemplifies loyalty and discipline. However, her experiences during the galaxy’s turbulent times challenge her allegiance, forcing her to confront the true cost of war and redefine her sense of duty. Skilled in both leadership and battlefield strategy, Iden balances the weight of her past with a hope for a better future.
