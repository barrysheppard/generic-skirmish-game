# Common Troopers

## Common Droids {#common-droids}

Non-combat droids are specialized machines designed to perform tasks ranging from translation and repair to logistics and medical care. Unlike battle droids, they lack weapons and combat training, focusing instead on support roles that serve both military and civilian needs across the galaxy.

Common droids are not designed for combat, but in a pinch can use their tools to defend themselves. It is assumed a droid in the Warband has had some minor reprogramming to allow them override their normal safety protocols. Any Warband may take up to two Droids.
