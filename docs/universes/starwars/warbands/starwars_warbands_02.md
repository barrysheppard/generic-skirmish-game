## **Imperial Stormtrooper** {#imperial-stormtrooper}

Stormtroopers are the Empire’s elite foot soldiers, trained to enforce order and crush rebellion across the galaxy. Clad in iconic white armor, they are disciplined, loyal, and relentless in their pursuit of Imperial objectives. 

| Fighter | Mv | Def | WS | W | Gear/Traits | Cr |
| :---- | :---: | :---: | :---: | :---: | :---- | :---: |
| **Stormtrooper**  | 4 | 5 | 3 | 12 | E-11 blaster rifle | 115 |
| **Stormtrooper** | 4 | 5 | 3 | 12 | DLT-19 Heavy Blaster | 130 |
| **Stormtrooper** | 4 | 5 | 3 | 12 | HH-12 Missile Launcher | 145 |
| **Stormtrooper Sergeant** | 4 | 5 | 4 | 14 | E-11 blaster rifle, Coordinated Strike, For the Empire\! | 140 |

A Stormtrooper will be equipped with an E-11 blaster rifle. If there are at least three stormtroopers then one of them will be a Stormtrooper Sergeant. If the warband has at least 5 Stormtroopers one non-sergeant can be equipped with a DLT-19 Heavy Blaster or HH-12 Missile Launcher instead of the E-11 blaster rifle. If the warband has 10 Stormtroopers, then the weapon which was not picked earlier can be given to a non-sergeant Stormtrooper instead of their E-11 blaster rifle.

| **Coordinated Strike** | \[double\] | Activate another friendly Stormtrooper after this fighter finishes their activation. |

Imperial Riot Control Squads are specialized security units deployed to suppress civil unrest, pacify rebellious populations, and enforce martial law on volatile worlds.

## **Imperial Shoretrooper** {#imperial-shoretrooper}

Shore Troopers are elite Imperial soldiers specially trained for combat in coastal and aquatic environments. Equipped with light armor and gear designed to withstand harsh weather and terrain, they excel in amphibious assaults and defense of strategic shoreline locations. 

| Fighter | Mv | Def | WS | W | Gear/Traits | Cr |
| :---- | :---: | :---: | :---: | :---: | :---- | :---: |
| **Shoretrooper** | 4 | 4 | 4 | 12 | E-22 Blaster Rifle | 135 |
| **Shore Trooper Sergeant** | 4 | 4 | 4 | 14 | E-22 Blaster Rifle, Coordinated Advance | 145 |
| **Shoretrooper** | 4 | 4 | 4 | 12 | T-21B Heavy Blaster | 145 |
| **Shoretrooper** | 4 | 4 | 4 | 12 | DF-90 Mortar, E-22 Blaster Rifle | 150 |

A Shoretrooper is typically equipped with an **E-22 blaster rifle**. If the warband includes at least two Shoretroopers, one of them can be designated as a **Shoretrooper Sergeant**. If the warband includes four or more Shoretroopers, one non-sergeant may replace their E-22 with a **T-21B Heavy Blaster** *or* a **DF-90 Mortar**. If the warband reaches the maximum squad size of six Shoretroopers, the second weapon option not already chosen may be assigned to a non-sergeant in place of their E-22.

| **DF-90 Mortar** | 6-24 | 4 | 1/2 | Immoble, Indirect Fire, Blast (3) |
