# Rebel Heroes


## **Cassian Andor** {#cassian-andor}

Cassian Andor is a seasoned intelligence officer and covert operative for the Rebel Alliance. Hardened by years of undercover missions and guerrilla warfare, he operates in the shadows to undermine the Empire’s control. Driven by a deep commitment to freedom and justice, Cassian often wrestles with the moral complexities of war, balancing ruthlessness with compassion. His resourcefulness and unwavering resolve make him a vital asset in the fight against tyranny.

If Cassian is selected for your Squad you may also select K-2SO.

K-2SO is a reprogrammed Imperial security droid turned Rebel ally, known for his blunt honesty and unwavering loyalty to Cassian Andor. With advanced combat and infiltration programming, he excels at both frontline support and covert operations. Despite his imposing stature and tactical prowess, K-2SO has a surprisingly dry sense of humor, often providing sarcastic commentary in the heat of battle. His reliability and quick thinking make him an invaluable companion in the fight against the Empire.

## **Iden Versio** {#iden-versio}

Iden Versio is an elite Imperial commander and the leader of Inferno Squad, the Empire’s most feared special forces unit. Trained from a young age in combat, espionage, and tactics, she exemplifies loyalty and discipline. However, her experiences during the galaxy’s turbulent times challenge her allegiance, forcing her to confront the true cost of war and redefine her sense of duty. Skilled in both leadership and battlefield strategy, Iden balances the weight of her past with a hope for a better future.

## **Ahsoka Tano** {#ahsoka-tano}

Once the Padawan of Anakin Skywalker, Ahsoka Tano carved her own path through the turmoil of the Clone Wars and beyond. Fierce, agile, and deeply principled, she left the Jedi Order after becoming disillusioned with its politics, yet continued to fight for justice across the galaxy. Wielding her signature white lightsabers and moving with precision and purpose, Ahsoka operates as a lone guardian — neither Jedi nor Sith, but something entirely her own.

## **Din Djarin – The Mandalorian** {#din-djarin-–-the-mandalorian}

Known simply as The Mandalorian or Mando, Din Djarin is a lone bounty hunter navigating the outer reaches of the galaxy in the wake of the Empire’s fall. Raised in the warrior culture of Mandalore and bound by the creed of his people, he is a skilled fighter, tracker, and survivor. While initially focused solely on completing contracts and upholding Mandalorian traditions, his path changes irrevocably upon meeting Grogu — the Force-sensitive child he chooses to protect. Over time, Mando becomes a reluctant hero, walking the line between mercenary and guardian, and facing down crime syndicates, Imperial remnants, and ancient foes, all while trying to define what it truly means to be Mandalorian.
