# Introduction

![Milky Way](./images/milky_way.jpg)


This Grimdark Future setting, is a distant future where humanity spans the stars under a vast, decaying empire of Man. It is an age of constant war, where countless worlds are beset by alien species, internal rebellion, and the corrupting forces of the Chaos.

Technology is ancient and poorly understood, treated as sacred rather than scientific, and progress has largely stagnated. Humanity is defended by powerful but rare warriors such as the Space Marines, alongside vast armies of ordinary soldiers.

Across the galaxy, numerous factions compete for survival or dominance, including brutal alien races and ancient civilizations, each locked in endless conflict. In this grim future, there is no peace, only war.


## Why GSG and not Kill Team?

There is of course another skirmish war game already set in the grimdark future, Killteam. There are a couple of reasons I've gone with the Warcry style core rules of GSG rather than a version of the Kill Team rule set. The main reason is the Warcry ruleset plays a little faster, mostly this is due to Killteams additional rolls and rerolls. So Killteam will typically have an attack roll, a defense roll, and some rerolls. Warcry in contrast has one single roll which should (in theory) factor in both sides at the same time.

I'm also looking for something a little more flexible. Killteam balancing is around the overall team, so you need to understand all the working parts of a team to balance them. In this game I want to mess around and mix fighters from different factions up if it makes sense for the story.
