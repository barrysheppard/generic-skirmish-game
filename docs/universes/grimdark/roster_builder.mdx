# Roster Builder

Select your fighters for your Roster and equip them from the armory.
The reference table at the bottom will automatically update to include the rules and stats for everything in your list.
Weapons and Gear are limited for each Fighter but if you want to mix and match, enable the House Rules checkbox to open up all options.

From your Roster, build your Warband for the battle.
Field your Fighters and assign them to one of the three battle groups (ABC).
If you find yourself over the points total, you can remove some weapons and gear in the Warband tab and when you Return the fighter after the battle, they'll get their old gear back.
