# Necrons

The Necrons are an ancient, undying race of cold machine-warriors whose metallic bodies have slumbered in stasis-tombs for millions of years. They now arise to reclaim their lost empire with advanced, soul-shredding gauss weaponry and a chilling disregard for all biological life.
