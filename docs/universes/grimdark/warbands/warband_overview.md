# Assembling your Warband

In this format, fighters are divided into Factions and each Fighter has a limited selection of Weapons, Gear, and Abilities. You must have at least one, and only one, Fighter with the 'Leader' trait. In many factions, Fighters will have the option to take the Leader trait or not.

The idea here is to have fun, so mixing some Fighter's between factions is encouraged for fun. Drop a single Intercessor in with a squad of Imperial Guard. Maybe don't have a Warband of Necrons, Drukhari, and Tyranids.

After a Warband has been assembled it should be split into three Deployment Groups: A, B, and C. Fighters in each Group deploy together. It is assumed that Group A is your primary Fire Warband and the suggested deployments have 4 in which Group A starts the game on the board. It would be a mistake to neglect Groups B and C however as in the remaining two deployments one of those two fire Warbands starts the game alone.
