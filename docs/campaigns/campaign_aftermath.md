# Aftermath Sequence

After each battle:

- Resolve [injuries](/genericskirmishgame/docs/campaigns/campaign_injuries) for taken-down fighters.
- Remove dead fighters from the roster.
- Gain **Points**, 100 for the win, 50 for a loss, and 10 for each [campaign treasure token](campaign_loop#campaign-treasure) carried by a member of your warband.
- Spend **Points** to buy new fighters, equipment, and [advancements](campaign_advancement).

Victory grants 100 points to spend on Fighters and Gear and a loss grants 50 points.

Points and Experience can be retained at this point, but can only be spent during this phase. As such, unspent Points and Experience cannot be spent until the Aftermath Squence of the next battle.
