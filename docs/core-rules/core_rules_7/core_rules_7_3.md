# **7.3 Fighter Activation** {#6.3-fighter-activation}

During the Combat Phase players will alternate activating their Fighters. A player can only activate one of their own fighters who has actions remaining.
When a fighter is activated, they become the focus of the game. Each turn a fighter can take two actions. Usually this happens during their activation. The same action can be taken twice, so you can Move then Move or Attack then Attack. Some missions may also add special actions, e.g. Pull the Lever. Once a fighter has finished taking actions they are given a token to indicate they cannot be activated again this turn.


# Standard Actions
| Name | Ability Cost | Effect |
| :---- | :----: | :---- |
| **Move** | \[action\] | Move up to the fighter's Movement characteristic in inches. A fighter within melee range of an enemy cannot move out of melee range with that enemy. |
| **Disengage** | \[action\] | Move up to 3" ending movement more than 1” away from all enemy fighters. |
| **Attack** | \[action\] | Make a melee or ranged attack with one of the fighter's weapon profiles. |
| **Wait** | \[action\] | No effect. The fighter may choose to end their activation, delaying any remaining actions for another activation later in the phase. |

Additional actions can be unlocked in the form of Abilities which can be given by Equipment or Fighter Traits.
