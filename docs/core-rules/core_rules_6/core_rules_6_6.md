# Twist

## **6.6 Twist** {#6.5-victory-&-twist}

If both players agree, randomly select a Twist. The twist is a special rule in effect for the game to add an extra element of chaos. This might be environmental such as a storm or darkness reducing the effectiveness of ranged attacks. It provides an extra challenge that the players must account for and increases the variety of games.

## 6.6.1 Twists


| 2D6 | Name  | Effect |
| :--: | :------------------- | :--- |
| 2 | **Night Raid** | Fighters can only see up to 8" for attack actions and abilites. |
| 3  | **Heavy Fog** | Add 1 to the Def characteristic of all fighters against ballistic skill attack actions. |
| 4  | **Uneven Ground** | Add 1 to the Def characteristic of fighters while they are within 1" of an obstacle. |
| 5  | **Defensive Position** | Add 1 to the Def characteristic of fighters while they are within 1" of another friendly fighter. |
| 6  | **Disciplined Advance** | Add 1 to the Move characteristic of all fighters during the first battle round. |
| 7  | **All Clear** | No twist is in effect for this battle. |
| 8  | **Lucky Break** | Players gain an extra wild die at the start of the combat. |
| 9  | **Favourable Winds** | Add 2" to the Range characteristic of ballistic skill attack actions (to a maximum of +3"). |
| 10  | **High Ground** | Add 1 to the Strength characteristic of attack actions made by fighters that are on a platform above their target. |
| 11 | **Battle Momentum**  | After taking down an enemy fighter, add 1 to the Move characteristic of that fighter until the end of their activation. |
| 12 | **Maximum Effort**   | Once per battle, a player can re-roll all hit rolls for one attack action. |
