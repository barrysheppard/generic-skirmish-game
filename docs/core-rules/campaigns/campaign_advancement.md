# 8.5 Fighter Advancement

Throughout a campaign, fighters will gain addition Gear which will boost their performance, but they can also gain improvements unlocking additional abilities and boosting their characteristics. Advancements can only be taken in the Aftermath sequence of a Mission and each Fighter can only be given one Advancement each Aftermath.


## Characteristic boosts

Like Talents, Characteristic boosts cost points. In this case it may reflect money spent on training. Unlike with Talents this does count towards the overall cost of the Fighter so it will increase the cost of the Fighter for Missions. Not all Characteristics can be boosted, only Weapon Skill, Ballistic Skill, Defence, and Wounds. The first advance for a characteristic costs 10 points with the second advance costing 20 points. Each Characteristic can only be advanced twice. For Wounds, each advance costs 20 and gives an additional 4 wounds.

| Name | M | WS | BS | Def | W | Traits | Cost |
| :---- | :---: | :---: | :---: | :---: | :---: | :---: | :---: |
| **Starting Captain** | 4 | 4 | 4 | 4 | 20 | Leader | 75 |
| **Advanced Captain** | 4 | 6 | 6 | 6 | 28 | Leader | 205 |

:::info Philosophy
If we want to try balance missions we need to include characteristic boosts.
:::

## Talents

Talents are Abilities which can be purchased for Fighters, typically only Leaders and Champions. They do cost Points to add them to your Fighter but they do not increase the cost of the Fighter. So they are a good way to improve your Fighter without needing to cut points elsewhere on the Warband.

The cost for a Talent is 20 points for each talent. This cost only applies to the Roster and doesn't apply when calculating points for the Warband.


| Name | M | WS | BS | Def | W | Traits | Cost |
| :---- | :---: | :---: | :---: | :---: | :---: | :--- | :---: |
| **Starting Captain** | 4 | 4 | 4 | 4 | 20 | Leader | 75 |
| **Talented Captain** | 4 | 4 | 4 | 4 | 20 | Leader, Dash, Quick Shot | 75/115 |

:::info Philosophy
An experience system was considered but ultimately was rejected to keep things simple. This way we have only a single resource for the Rosters making it easier to track. The talents are intended to be Abilities so they will be locked under the Ability Dice system.
:::
