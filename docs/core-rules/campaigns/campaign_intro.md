# 8. Introduction to Campaign Rules (Narrative Play)

Narrative play links multiple Missions into a campaign where your Warband evolves over time. Each Mission impacts future games through progression, injuries, and rewards. Each player keeps track of a Roster which can gain additional fighters and can advance. At the start of a Mission, players put together Warbands from their Rosters with roughly the same points cost. This should mean battles between two Warbands will balanced even if one player is brand new and the other has played dozens of games with this Roster. Playing through a Campaign will expand out the Roster and hopefully will make for an engaging story while still keeping games between players fun.
