# Ability Reference

This list is a best effort to summarise some of the main types of ability within the game as a reference point to start from.

## Bonus Move or Attack
| Name | Ability Cost | Effect |
|:-----|:-----|:------------|
| Blood for the Blood God | [double] | A fighter can only use this ability if an enemy fighter has been taken down by an attack action made by them this activation. This fighter makes a bonus move action or a bonus attack action. |

## Control / Lockdown

| Name | Ability Cost | Effect |
|:-----|:-----|:------------|
| Distant Lockdown | [double] | Pick an enemy fighter within 20" of this fighter. Until the end of the Mission round, that fighter cannot make disengage actions. |
| Engagement Aura | [double] | Until the end of the Mission round, enemy fighters cannot make disengage actions while within 1" of this fighter.|
| Marshal's Net | [double] | Pick a visible enemy fighter within 3" of this fighter and roll a dice. On a 3+, that fighter cannot make move actions or disengage actions this round. |
| Mesmerise | [double] | Pick an enemy fighter within a number of inches of this fighter equal to the value of this ability and roll a dice. On a roll of 3+, until the end of the Mission round, that fighter cannot make move actions or disengage actions. |

## Damage Mitigation

| Name | Ability Cost | Effect |
|:-----|:-----|:------------|
| Evasive Reflex | [action] | Reaction. A fighter can make this reaction when they are targeted by an attack action but before the hit rolls are made. Subtract 1 from the damage points allocated to this fighter by each hit from that attack action (to a minimum of 1). |
| Reactive Recovery | [action] | Reaction. A fighter can make this reaction after they are targeted by a melee attack action but before the hit rolls are made. Remove 3 damage points allocated to this fighter. |
| Sturdy Stance | [double] | Until the end of the Mission round, subtract 1 from the damage points allocated by each hit and critical hit (to a minimum of 1) from attack actions that target this figther. |
