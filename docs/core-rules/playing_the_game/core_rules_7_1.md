# Playing the Game

## 7.1 Game Structure {#7.1-game-structure}

After setup, a game consists of 4 Mission rounds. Each round has four phases. At the end of the fourth round, victory points are calculated to determine who the winner of the battle is. In campaign mode, there will be additional rolls to make after the end of the game to track campaign progress.

    - **Game Setup**
    - **Mission round 1**
    - **Mission round 2**
    - **Mission round 3**
    - **Mission round 4**
    - **Game Conclusion**
